Noida FAQs

Short answers for people searching Noida MLA and election result information.

Who won Noida Assembly election in 2022?

Pankaj Singh of BJP won Noida in 2022 with 2,44,319 votes and 70.16% vote share.

Who was runner-up in Noida in 2022?

Sunil Choudhary of SP was runner-up in Noida in 2022 with 62,806 votes and 18.04% vote share.

Which party won Noida in 2012?

BJP won Noida in 2012. The winning candidate was Mahesh Kumar Sharma.
