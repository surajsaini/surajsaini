Jewar FAQs

Short answers for people searching Jewar MLA and election result information.

Who won Jewar Assembly election in 2022?

Dhirendra Singh of BJP won Jewar in 2022 with 1,17,205 votes and 50.53% vote share.

Who was runner-up in Jewar in 2022?

Avtar Singh Bhadana of RLD was runner-up in Jewar in 2022 with 60,890 votes and 26.25% vote share.

Which party won Jewar in 2012?

BSP won Jewar in 2012. The winning candidate was Vedram Bhati.
