Ward Parshad

Ghaziabad Nagar Nigam is divided into 100 wards. Each ward elects one ward parshad (ward councilor or corporator) to represent their specific neighborhood in the city council.

Ward parshad representatives handle local issues like road repairs, sanitation, streetlights, and other civic amenities in their area.
