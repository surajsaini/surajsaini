General Elections (Lok Sabha)

National-level elections to choose a Member of Parliament (MP) who represents Ghaziabad in the Lok Sabha (lower house of India's Parliament).

Constituency: Ghaziabad Lok Sabha (No. 12)

Voters in Ghaziabad elect one MP every 5 years to represent them in the Parliament of India. The MP works on national issues like law-making, budget, and policy.
