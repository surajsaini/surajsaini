Final Result

Egypt came from behind for a historic 3-1 win

Egypt defeated New Zealand 3-1 at BC Place Stadium on Sunday, June 21, 2026. Finn Surman gave New Zealand a first-half lead, but Egypt answered after halftime through Mostafa Zico, Mohamed Salah, and Trezeguet.

The result was Egypt's first-ever FIFA World Cup win and moved the Pharaohs to the top of Group G on four points. New Zealand remains on one point before its June 26 match against Belgium in Vancouver.
