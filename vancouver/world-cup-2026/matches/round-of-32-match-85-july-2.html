What is the Round of 32?

The 2026 FIFA World Cup is the first tournament with 48 teams. This expanded format introduces a new Round of 32 between the group stage and the Round of 16.

How the Knockout Stage Works

  • 48 teams compete in 12 groups of 4 teams each
  • The top 2 teams from each group advance (24 teams)
  • The best 8 third-place teams also advance (8 teams)
  • 32 teams enter the Round of 32 knockout stage
  • Winners advance to the Round of 16, then Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, and Final
