Assembly Elections (Vidhan Sabha)

State-level elections to elect Members of Legislative Assembly (MLA) to the Uttar Pradesh Vidhan Sabha. Ghaziabad district has 6 assembly constituencies.

Assembly Constituencies in Ghaziabad District:

Loni (53), Muradnagar (54), Sahibabad (55), Ghaziabad (56), Dholana (58), and Modinagar.

Voters elect one MLA from their constituency every 5 years. MLAs work on state issues in the UP Legislative Assembly.
