BC Place Stadium Vancouver

BC Place Stadium is one of the most famous sports venues in Canada. It will host 7 FIFA World Cup 2026 matches including this exciting Group B clash between host nation Canada and Qatar.

Stadium Facts

  • Seating Capacity: 54,500 for FIFA World Cup matches
  • Roof Type: Retractable roof (covered stadium)
  • Surface: Artificial turf (temporary natural grass for World Cup)
  • Opened: 1983
  • Location: Downtown Vancouver, British Columbia

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does Canada vs Qatar start in Vancouver?

The match kicks off at 3:00 PM Pacific Time on Thursday, June 18, 2026 at BC Place Stadium.

Is this a home game for Canada?

Yes! As one of the three host nations (alongside USA and Mexico), Canada will play group stage matches on home soil. This match in Vancouver is expected to have a strong Canadian atmosphere.

How early should I arrive at BC Place?

For a Canada match, we recommend arriving at least 2.5-3 hours early. The atmosphere outside the stadium will be incredible, and you won't want to miss a moment of this historic occasion.

Will tickets for Canada matches sell out?

Very likely! Host nation matches are always in high demand. Sign up for ticket notifications on the official FIFA website as soon as possible.

What is the record between Canada and Qatar?

Qatar beat Canada 1-0 in the 2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup semi-final. This match gives Canada a chance for revenge on the biggest stage in football.

Can I bring a flag or banner to support Canada?

Small flags on non-rigid poles are usually allowed. Large banners may require prior approval from FIFA. Check the official FIFA fan guide for specific rules.
