Head coach Jesse Marsch and his staff are actively evaluating players through camps and friendly matches. FIFA rules require a provisional roster of 35-55 players to be submitted first, followed by the final 26-man squad closer to the tournament kickoff.

Vancouver's Role in World Cup 2026

Vancouver will be a central hub for the tournament, hosting seven matches at BC Place stadium, including two of Canada's group stage games.

Fans in Vancouver are eagerly awaiting the opportunity to cheer on the national team on home soil. The city is focusing on refining logistics like safety, security, and traffic plans for the massive influx of visitors.

The excitement is building as Canada prepares to host and compete in the World Cup for only the second time in history.
