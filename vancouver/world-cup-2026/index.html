Transportation Options

🚗 Arriving by Car

Parking: Must be pre-purchased; no on-site match-day payments.

Driving is not recommended because road closures and local-traffic-only restrictions will be in effect around BC Place and False Creek.

🚇 Recommended SkyTrain

Station: Main Street-Science World

Use this station on match days for the signed Last Mile route to the BC Place entrance at Carrall Street and Pacific Boulevard.

🚇 Downtown SkyTrain Access

Station: Yaletown/Roundhouse

Yaletown-Roundhouse and Stadium-Chinatown remain open, but direct pedestrian access to BC Place will be limited on match days.

🚆 West Coast Express

Station: Waterfront

Special weekend round trips are planned for June 13 and June 21, with an eastbound train after the match.

🚌 Bus/Transit

Extra service: About 600 additional bus trips per day during the tournament.

Expect more service on downtown routes R5, 14, 19, and 23, plus shuttles to the FIFA Fan Festival at Hastings Park.

⛴️ SeaBus

Route: North Shore to Waterfront

SeaBus runs every 15 minutes or better during the tournament and every 10 minutes before and after BC Place matches.

🚲 Bike, Roll, or Walk

Bike valet: Near Main Street-Science World and the FIFA Fan Festival.

Walking, cycling, rolling, bike share, and scooter share are recommended for short downtown trips.

🎉 FIFA Fan Festival

Location: Hastings Park / PNE grounds

Dedicated transit shuttles will connect the festival grounds with both the Expo and Millennium lines.

✈️ Vancouver International Airport

Transit Time: Under 30 minutes to downtown by Canada Line SkyTrain

Follow airport signs for YVR-Airport Station between the international and domestic terminals.
