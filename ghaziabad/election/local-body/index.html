Ward Councilors: The city has 100 wards. Each ward elects one corporator to represent their neighborhood.

Ward Councilors

Ghaziabad Municipal Corporation is divided into 100 wards. Each ward elects one corporator (ward councilor) to represent their specific neighborhood in the city council.

Ward councilors handle local issues like road repairs, sanitation, streetlights, and other civic amenities in their area.
