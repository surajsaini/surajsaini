Frequently Asked Questions

What time does Switzerland vs Canada start in Vancouver?

The match kicks off at 12:00 Noon Pacific Time on Wednesday, June 24, 2026 at BC Place Stadium.

Why is this match important for Canada?

This is Canada's third and final group stage match. The result will likely determine if Canada advances to the knockout rounds of their home World Cup.

How strong is Switzerland at the World Cup?

Switzerland is very consistent. They have reached the Round of 16 at four consecutive World Cups (2006, 2014, 2018, 2022) and are a tough opponent for any team.

Will there be special fan activities before the match?

Yes, FIFA typically organizes Fan Festivals and activities around World Cup matches. Expect the area around BC Place to be filled with fans celebrating before kickoff.

Can I get to the match during my lunch break?

The match starts at noon on a Wednesday. If you work downtown, you may be able to attend, but plan to arrive early as getting through security takes time.

Is parking available near BC Place?

Yes, but we strongly recommend using public transit. The Stadium-Chinatown SkyTrain station is very close to BC Place. Parking will be expensive and limited during World Cup matches.
