Ghaziabad Nagar Nigam

Mayor: Directly elected by city residents every 5 years.

Current Mayor: Sunita Dayal (BJP)

Ward Parshad: Each of the 100 wards elects one ward parshad, also called a ward councilor, to represent its neighborhood.
