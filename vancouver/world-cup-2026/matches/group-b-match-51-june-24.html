Final Result

Switzerland won Group B, Canada still advanced

Switzerland defeated Canada 2-1 at BC Place Stadium on Wednesday, June 24, 2026. Ruben Vargas scored just after halftime, Johan Manzambi doubled the lead, and Promise David pulled one back for Canada in the 75th minute.

Switzerland finished first in Group B on seven points and stays in Vancouver for the Round of 32. Canada finished second on four points and advances to face South Africa in Los Angeles on June 28.
