BC Place Stadium Vancouver

BC Place Stadium will host this exciting Group G clash between New Zealand and Egypt. The covered stadium offers a fantastic atmosphere for this match between two nations from different football confederations.

Stadium Facts

  • Seating Capacity: 54,500 for FIFA World Cup matches
  • Roof Type: Retractable roof (covered stadium)
  • Surface: Artificial turf (temporary natural grass for World Cup)
  • Opened: 1983
  • Location: Downtown Vancouver, British Columbia

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does New Zealand vs Egypt start in Vancouver?

The match kicks off at 6:00 PM Pacific Time on Sunday, June 21, 2026 at BC Place Stadium.

Will Mohamed Salah play in this match?

Barring injury, Mohamed Salah is expected to be Egypt's star player and captain at the 2026 World Cup. He is the main reason many fans want to attend Egypt matches.

When did New Zealand last play at a World Cup?

New Zealand's last World Cup was in 2010 in South Africa. They famously went undefeated, drawing all three group stage matches 1-1 including against Italy.

How early should I arrive at BC Place?

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before kick-off. This gives you time to pass through security, find your seat, and enjoy the pre-match atmosphere.

Is BC Place covered?

Yes, BC Place has a retractable roof so you will be comfortable regardless of whether it rains outside.

Are food and drinks available inside the stadium?

Yes, BC Place has food and beverage outlets on Level 2 and Level 4. Expect longer lines during World Cup matches, so plan accordingly.
