Dadri FAQs

Short answers for people searching Dadri MLA and election result information.

Who won Dadri Assembly election in 2022?

Tejpal Singh Nagar of BJP won Dadri in 2022 with 2,18,068 votes and 61.64% vote share.

Who was runner-up in Dadri in 2022?

Rajkumar Bhati of SP was runner-up in Dadri in 2022 with 79,850 votes and 22.57% vote share.

Which party won Dadri in 2012?

BSP won Dadri in 2012. The winning candidate was Satveer Singh Gurjar.
