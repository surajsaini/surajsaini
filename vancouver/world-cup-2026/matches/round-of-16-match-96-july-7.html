Frequently Asked Questions

What time does Round of 16 Match 96 start in Vancouver?

The match kicks off at 1:00 PM Pacific Time on Tuesday, July 7, 2026 at BC Place Stadium.

Is this the last World Cup match in Vancouver?

Yes, this Round of 16 match is the final FIFA World Cup 2026 game at BC Place Stadium. After this, the tournament continues at other host cities.

What happens after the Round of 16?

The winner of this match advances to the Quarter-finals. From there, winning teams progress through Semi-finals to the Final in New York/New Jersey on July 19, 2026.

Are Round of 16 tickets more expensive than group stage?

Yes, knockout stage tickets are typically more expensive. The Round of 16 is the biggest match in Vancouver and will command premium prices.

What happens if the match is tied?

In knockout matches, there must be a winner. If tied after 90 minutes, matches go to 30 minutes of extra time. If still tied, there's a dramatic penalty shootout.

Can I take time off work for this match?

The match is on a Tuesday at 1:00 PM. Many fans will take the afternoon off to attend. Plan ahead and request time off early!
