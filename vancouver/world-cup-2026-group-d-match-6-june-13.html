BC Place Stadium Vancouver

BC Place Stadium is one of the most famous sports venues in Canada. It will host 7 FIFA World Cup 2026 matches. The stadium is located in downtown Vancouver and offers a fantastic experience for soccer fans from around the world.

Stadium Facts

  • Seating Capacity: 54,500 for FIFA World Cup matches
  • Roof Type: Retractable roof (covered stadium)
  • Surface: Artificial turf (temporary natural grass for World Cup)
  • Opened: 1983
  • Location: Downtown Vancouver, British Columbia

Getting to BC Place

BC Place is easy to reach by public transit. The Stadium-Chinatown SkyTrain station is just a 7-minute walk away. For detailed directions including parking, transit, and walking routes, visit our main guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the Australia vs D4 match start in Vancouver?

The match kicks off at 9:00 PM Pacific Time on Saturday, June 13, 2026 at BC Place Stadium.

Is BC Place a covered stadium?

Yes, BC Place has a retractable roof. This means fans can enjoy the match in comfort regardless of weather conditions outside.

How early should I arrive at BC Place for the World Cup match?

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before kick-off. Security checks for FIFA World Cup matches are thorough, and you will want time to find your seat and enjoy the atmosphere.

Can I bring bags into BC Place?

Small bags under 12" x 6" x 12" are allowed. Large bags, backpacks, and luggage are not permitted. Check the official FIFA and BC Place guidelines before attending.

Is this match sold out?

Ticket sales have not yet started. Sign up on the official FIFA ticketing website to receive notifications when tickets become available.

Are children allowed at FIFA World Cup matches?

Yes, children of all ages can attend. Children under 3 may enter free if they sit on an adult's lap, but it's recommended to have a ticket for everyone for comfort.
