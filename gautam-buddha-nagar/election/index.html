How many Assembly seats are in Gautam Buddha Nagar?

Who won Noida Assembly election in 2022?

Pankaj Singh of BJP won the Noida Assembly election in 2022 with 2,44,319 votes and 70.16% vote share.

Who won Dadri Assembly election in 2022?

Tejpal Singh Nagar of BJP won the Dadri Assembly election in 2022 with 2,18,068 votes and 61.64% vote share.

Who won Jewar Assembly election in 2022?
